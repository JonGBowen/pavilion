Welcome to the Connector game!

This game is played by two human players (you and another person).

In each round, you either play as a SPEAKER or as an GUESSER. You are the Speaker in this game, and you will play with a Guesser. You will be shown two highlighted words on the board above, and your task is to come up with a one-word clue that is related to both words. This clue should be informative to the Guesser, so that they can guess the two words on the board based on the clue

The game is repeated a certain number of rounds, and each round roles are randomly assigned.

If you understood the instructions correctly press the "DONE" button to proceed to the game.
